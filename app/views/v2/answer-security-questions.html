Answer security questions

We'll ask you some security questions that only you should know the answers to. This will help us stop anyone else who might have your details from pretending to be you.

The questions will be about things like your mobile phone contract, bank account, and any credit cards, loans or mortgages you have.

! Warning Try to answer these questions as accurately as you can. You will not be able to change your answers if you make a mistake.

We will not keep your answers.

How we know what questions to ask you

We'll check your details with Experian (opens in new tab). This is because companies like Experian have access to information about interactions you've previously had with other organisations.

Read our privacy notice (opens in new tab) if you'd like to know more about how your details will be used to prove your identity.
